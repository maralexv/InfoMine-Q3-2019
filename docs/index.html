Maximising production yields while keeping safety and environmental impact under control.

Managing beneficiation based on target element concentrations is a routine task. Achieving it with high precision, reliability and repeatability is far from trivial. A well-equiped laboratory would certainly do that. However, in this case a Process engineer would wait for hours before the results from his QA colleagues are available.

Similar issues exist in steel making, where technological process stabilisation is more and more difficult with growing variations in the quality of raw material inputs. Sinter mix basicity variations increase the consumption of expensive coke and energy.

Grade and recovery assay-based control are commonplace in copper and molybdenum industries. But what if one could start sorting ores before they enter beneficiation process? That would help better regulate the amount of flotation chemicals, collectors and frothers and lead to savings and better production yields.

MAYA™ Online Analyser provides a solution to these kinds of problems, enabling automated control of elemental composition without sample preparation and in real time.
